What is Reselling?

Reselling is the art and business of buying popular products at a low or wholesale price, then selling them for a higher price to retail buyers or collectors. Whether it’s limited-edition electronics, designer fragrances, or hyped streetwear, reselling lets you turn your phone into a source of cash flow.

Anyone can do it—no experience or big upfront investment required. Learn the secrets to sourcing, listing, and selling like a pro with JRC.
